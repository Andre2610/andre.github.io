Who am I?

Hello! My name is Andre, I am 29 years old! Born and raised in Lisbon, Portugal but I have lived in a few different countries in Europe, with a 4-month stint in Atlanta, GA as well.
I worked as a flight attendant for over 5 years but quit in November 2018, to re-think my goals and priorities while traveling for a while.
I love moving around and give myself the chance to be immersed in the local culture and customs and hopefully grasp some of the language too. A big plus if I end up in a place that allows me to do some scuba diving!

My goal!

I decided on a career change to become a developer in August 2019. I tried the self-taught route in my spare time, but given the current situation in the world, I figured this was the best time to commit to learning code full-time with a structured curriculum instead.
Looking forward to meet you all and tackle this challenging course!
